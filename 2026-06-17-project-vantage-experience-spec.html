The Experience Spec — v1 — 2026-06-17

Project Vantage

Peter's personal operating system — what it must feel like to work.

This document defines the standard, not the system. Each pillar names how the work should feel — not what is built. Return at each benchmark date and score the system against this spec honestly.

Pillar I

Peace

Not the absence of challenges — complete confidence in handling them, and a positive mood about them.

  • Everything handled — obligations, commitments, legal requirements tracked, anticipated, executed in time.
  • Active team watching on Peter's behalf, proactively flagging where to focus.
  • Early warning — nothing catches Peter off-guard; new developments scanned for cross-domain impact.
  • Stall detection — nothing slips through; stated commitments with no action are surfaced.
  • Wins acknowledged — progress is felt, not just problems.
  • Belief maintained — Peter stays in a confident, positive state.
  • It's working — financially, relationally, creatively.
Pillar II

Power

Outstanding work produced in less time. What was impossible alone becomes possible.

  • Superpowers — the team extends what Peter can do and be.
  • Active environmental intelligence — fed what he needs to know when he needs it.
  • Curated information diet — time to read, think, and produce is protected.
Pillar III

Purpose

Peter knows what he does. He is doing his life's work. The right people are finding him.

  • Identity answered — authentically, not rehearsed.
  • Mission being fulfilled — living his life's work.
  • Right people seeking him out — those who share his values and humour; not afraid to take anyone's call.
Foundation

Living System

Not a pillar — the ground all three stand on.

Self-aware, self-regulating, Peter as its primary concern. Knows itself: the vault, the apps, the models, the hardware, the world outside. Learns and adapts continuously — not in response to failures Peter experiences, but as a standing discipline. Gets better without Peter having to make it better. Ensures Peace does not erode, Power does not plateau, and Purpose does not drift.
