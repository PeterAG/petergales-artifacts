Remembering App — 1-Page Plan

Completed Nov 2024 · IWAI 1-Page Idea Plan framework (Innovating with AI, founder Rob Howard)
Per Peter, this is the best existing description of the Remembering App idea — what it is, the problem it solves, the benefit gained, for whom, and why he's qualified to deliver it.

Combining AI with relevant/borrowed expertise in

Personal Development.

This product/process merges

Traditional tools — books, podcasts, articles, videos; a person's mission, values, statement, affirmations, and other positive content sources.

With AI.

To help

Primary audience: Purpose/Identity Seekers and legacy builders.

Solve problem

  • Helps users resist the pull of unfulfilling cultural values by offering structured reminders of their mission and legacy, fostering a sense of connection and purpose in daily life.
  • Helps users combat feelings of emptiness and disconnection by offering structured reminders of their mission, values, and other positive content.

So they'll be able to

  • Feel more centered, purposeful, less stressed.
  • Make better decisions.

In just

Minutes a day. Once set up, it's an automation — on rising and before bed.

Here's how it works

Positive personal content → AI → structured reminders throughout the day.
